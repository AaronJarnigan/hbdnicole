Dusty Old Album

Possibly the first picture you ever posted of us. Taken at the lot on Maynardville Highway.
Simple days mean as much as the special ones.
Making sure I get to do something I think is cool.
Exploring Chattanooga on another random day.
Wandering through the woods like teenagers with no destination.
Wandering up the mountain on a rainy cool day.
A day we'll never replicate again.
Sharing our time with people that mean the most.
Reunited with old friends--and yeah, it felt good.
I see your best friend.
My two favorite.
Weekends we'll never forget.
